    What makes Kerala so special?

    The beaches,the hills,the beautiful green
    landscape,the list goes on and on.
    From the enormous lakes like Ashtamudi
    to the drenched rainforests of Silent Vally,
    Kerala is a perfect example of Silent Vally,
    Kerala is a perfect example of what is to be
    known as boundles beauty of nature.
    Blessed with several thousand species of
    plant,animal and birds,the biodiversity that
    Kerala has to offer is much greater than any
    other state of India
